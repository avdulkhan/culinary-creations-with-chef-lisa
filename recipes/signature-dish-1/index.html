Delight your taste buds with Chef Lisa's most celebrated dish!

Garlic Butter Lobster Tail

Chef Lisa's signature dish, the Garlic Butter Lobster Tail, is an exquisite combination of fresh lobster, delicately grilled and coated with a rich garlic butter sauce, served with a side of crispy roasted vegetables.

Ingredients: Lobster tail, garlic, butter, lemon, fresh herbs, and a touch of salt and pepper.

Preparation Time: 25 minutes
